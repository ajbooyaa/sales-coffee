Our Story

Born in Seattle

Sales Coffee Shop started with a simple belief: everyone deserves an exceptional cup of coffee. We source our beans directly from small farms, roast them locally, and train our baristas with the care and precision of a craft.

Whether you're here for a quick espresso before work or a slow afternoon with your laptop, we're glad you're here. This is your coffee shop.
